## About me

I am an economist holding a **Ph.D. in Economics** from Université Clermont Auvergne and Université d'Orléans (CERDI · LEO), and currently a **Postdoctoral Researcher** at **BETA – INRAE** in Nancy, France. My work sits at the intersection of **macroeconomics, environmental and resource economics, and international economy**, with a strong empirical orientation — panel econometrics, causal inference, and policy impact evaluation.

Beyond academic research, I am interested in **nowcasting and short-term economic forecasting from alternative data** (satellite imagery, web scraping, Google Trends, NLP) for public and private decision-makers. This GitHub account is the home of my open research code, replication packages, and the first demonstrators of the Quanti portfolio.

---

## Research interests

- Macroeconomic and sectoral nowcasting; mixed-frequency models (DFM, MIDAS, BVAR, state-space)
- Alternative data for economic measurement: night-time lights, web scraping, Google Trends, NLP
- Natural resource economics — mining, forests, land use
- International trade, Global Value Chains, economic integration
- Institutions, governance, and public policy in developing countries
